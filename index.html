How to calculate percentage — basic formula

The basic percentage formula is:

Percentage = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100

For example: if you scored 450 out of 600, your percentage is (450 ÷ 600) × 100 = 75%.

How to find X% of a number

X% of Y = (X ÷ 100) × Y

Example: 20% of 500 = (20 ÷ 100) × 500 = 100.

How to calculate percentage increase

Percentage Increase = ((New Value – Old Value) ÷ Old Value) × 100

Example: salary increased from ₹50,000 to ₹65,000 = ((65,000 – 50,000) ÷ 50,000) × 100 = 30% increase.

How to calculate percentage decrease / discount

Discounted Price = Original Price × (1 – Discount% ÷ 100)

Example: ₹2,000 item with 25% discount = 2,000 × (1 – 0.25) = ₹1,500.
